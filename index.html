Our Story

Loom & Luxe started with a simple idea: African fabrics deserve more than just being materials. They're stories woven into cloth, generations of artistry passed down through skilled hands.

I'm Stephanie, and I grew up surrounded by these textiles. My grandmother would drape them over her shoulders like armor, each pattern telling a different story. When I couldn't find quality fabrics locally, I decided to bridge that gap myself.

Every piece here is handpicked. I work directly with artisans who've mastered their craft over decades, ensuring what you get is authentic and made to last. No mass production, no shortcuts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions we receive from our customers.

What is your refund policy?

We offer refunds for eligible purchases. If you're not satisfied with your fabric or if there's an issue with your order, please contact us within 7 days of receipt. We'll work with you to resolve any concerns and process refunds for qualifying cases.

Can you source specific materials for me?

Absolutely! We specialize in sourcing authentic African fabrics and can help you find specific materials, patterns, or designs. Just tell us what you're looking for in your message, and we'll work with our network of artisans to find exactly what you need.

How do I know the fabric quality before ordering?

All our fabrics are handpicked for quality and authenticity. We work directly with skilled artisans who've mastered their craft over decades. While we can't send samples for every fabric, we're happy to provide detailed descriptions and answer any specific questions about texture, weight, and durability.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location. For customers in Nigeria, delivery typically takes 2-5 business days. International shipping usually takes 7-14 business days, depending on your country and customs processing. We'll provide you with a more specific timeline when you place your order.

Do you offer custom fabric orders?

Yes! We can arrange custom fabric orders through our network of artisans. Whether you need specific colors, patterns, or quantities, we can work with you to create exactly what you envision. Custom orders may take longer to fulfill, but we'll keep you updated throughout the process.

What makes your fabrics different from others?

Our fabrics are sourced directly from African artisans, ensuring authenticity and quality. We don't use mass production or shortcuts - every piece is carefully selected for its craftsmanship and cultural significance. We also support sustainable practices and fair trade with local communities.

How do I care for my African fabrics?

African fabrics require gentle care to maintain their beauty and longevity. We recommend hand washing in cold water with mild detergent, avoiding bleach and harsh chemicals. Air dry away from direct sunlight to prevent fading. For delicate pieces, consider professional dry cleaning. We'll include care instructions with every order.
